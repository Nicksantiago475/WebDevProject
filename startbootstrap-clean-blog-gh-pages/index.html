How to Save Up for those Rainy Days of Life

Life is Full of Rainy Days

Bad things happen in life. No matter who we are or where we come from, bad things or "Rainy Days" will happen to us. It is our job to be prepared for these dreadful moments in life.

What is a Rainy Day Fund?

A reserved amount of money to be used in times when regular income is disrupted or decreased by unfortunate events. This reserve is called the Rainy Day Fund.

Why do we need a Rainy Day Fund?

Ever had your car break down, lost your job unexpectedly, or even found your house on fire? Having a Rainy Day Fund will help alleviate these unfortunate events. Anything can happen in this life. Putting a portion of our income to this fund will help prepare us for anything that can ruin us financially.

Save up to Save Lives

Although it may seem daunting or a nuisance to put your money in another savings acount, this savings account could end up saving your life in times of need. Click on the 'Rainy Day Steps' tab to learn more and start creating your Rainy Day Fund.
